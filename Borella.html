An attractive three bedroom semi-detached family home situated within 0.5 miles of Borella station with trains and within easy walking distance of local shops, schools, bus routes and Nations Trust Bank. The property comprises; two receptions, fitted 18'9 x 10'1 kitchen/breakfast room and conservatory. The property also benefits from having a utility room and cloakroom. To the first floor there are three bedrooms and a family bathroom with separate WC. Additional features include double glazing, gas central heating and a well presented interior.
